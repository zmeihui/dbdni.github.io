Database Lab Research Seminar

Announcements

If you are interested in database research, you are welcome to join us in our weekly database seminar. Every week a local or visiting researcher gives a talk on his/her research topic.

Location/Time: The seminar is held on Fridays 2:00-3:30pm in CSE 3217.

Course Number: If you are a student and plan to attend, please enroll in CSE 239A.

Mailing List: Announcements about the seminar are sent to the DBTalks mailing list. To (un)subscribe, please click here.
